I’m Piero Romare, PhD Student in Usable Privacy and Security at Chalmers University of Technology, Gothenburg (Sweden), supervised by prof. Simone Fischer-Hübner in the WASP Graduate School.

I’ve collaborated with the SPRITZ Security and Privacy Research Group led by prof. Mauro Conti and with the Ubiquitous Interaction Research Group at the University of Helsinki supervised by prof. Giulio Jacucci.

My work is mostly about Season 6, Ep 1, Black Mirror IMDB

  • Usable Privacy on IoT Trigger-Action Platform
  • User-friendly Authentication Methods
  • Human-centric cybersecurity on IoT Wearable
