Piero Romare

Piero Romare is a PhD Student in Usable Privacy and Security at Chalmers University of Technology, Gothenburg (Sweden), supervised by prof. Simone Fischer-Hübner, co-supervised by prof. Morten Fjeld and Dr. Farzaneh Karegar funded by WASP Wallenberg AI, Autonomous Systems and Software Program.

He obtained a bachelor degree in Cognitive Psychology at the Department of General Psychology and a master degree in Data Science at the Department of Mathematics from the University of Padua (Italy).

He has collaborated with SPRITZ Security and Privacy Research Group led by prof. Mauro Conti and with Ubiquitous Interaction Research Group at the University of Helsinki (Finland) led by prof. Giulio Jacucci.
