Past Lives

MasieraDay

Masieraday is an association established for charitable purposes in memory of Livio Romare, a Schio volleyball champion who always had a thought for the community and young people, leading him to work personally for the good of those less fortunate.
Masieraday is under the patronage of the Italian Ministry of Education, University and Research.
MasieraDay (ENG) - MasieraDay (IT)
  • Since 2015, organization of volleyball matches with MasieraDay.
  • Since 2017, organization of volleyball tournaments with MasieraCup.
  • Since 2018, donation of 5 scholarships each year with MasieraAcademy.
Thanks to the donations, aid has been devolved to Adwa Ethiopia, Vita Onlus project, AViLL (association against leukemia and lymphomas), Burundi Chiama (long distance adoptions).
On 5th December 2015 the Schio sports hall was named Palasport Livio Romare.
You can relive the association's events organised via the YouTube channel.

Proiem

During the years of high school I started a online shop. I was a reseller of streetwear brands including Supreme, Palace, Stussy, Huf, Obey. I experimented with coding as a self-teacher to build the website and the recommendation system. I closed it after 2 years because I had to focus on my studies at the university. My friend Filippo helped me with the logo.

Single Speed Bike

I have always been passionate about cycling. I have been riding a bike since I was a child. I have always been fascinated by the world of fixed gear bikes. I bought my first fixed gear bike in 2012. I built it with my father. I bought the frame and the components separately and I assembled them. I have been riding it for many years and many accidents happened, but I didn't like the brakes.

Music

I have always been passionate about music. I started around the end of middle school and I ran a techno project alone under the name of Peter Niemi. A few embarrassing songs should still be on Spotify.
With my friend Stefan, we started a duo of electronic music called ThElements. We had a lot of fun. Together with my friend Andrea who played the electronic drums, we played a few times live using the name ThElements of Live.
This was my home studio. My setup was with a Native Instruments Traktor S4, a Novation Launchpad, and an Akai MPK25. I'm also a vinyl collector.

Peter Hall Tv
More than 10 years ago, with my friends Filippo, Federico, and Andrea, we created a YouTube channel called Peter Hall Tv. During that time, we streamed online techno music. We were one of the first to do it in Italy thanks also to Youtube which at that time did not ask for a certain amount of subscribers to allow the streaming. We streamed every two weeks, on Sunday evenings, by hosting several DJs from our city.

"PETER HALL is a project born out of a passion for underground music, we decided to give space to young artists in the area who want to share their sound online and live, thus allowing other music and dancefloor enthusiasts to confront each other and have fun discovering new sounds and trends, passion is fundamental in Peter Hall TV, living music to the fullest seeks".
