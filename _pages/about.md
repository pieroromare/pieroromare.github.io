Piero Romare is a [WASP](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BZCqRmQUbIk) PhD Student in Usable Privacy and Security at [Chalmers University of Technology](https://www.chalmers.se), Gothenburg (Sweden), supervised by [prof. Simone Fisher-Hübner](https://www.kau.se/forskare/simone-fischer-hubner) in the [WASP Graduate School](https://wasp-sweden.org). <br>
Piero has a bachelor's degree in Cognitive Psychoology and a master's degree in Data Science at [Univeristy of Padua](https://www.unipd.it), Padua (Italy). <br>
He collaborated with the [SPRITZ Security and Privacy Research Group](https://spritz.math.unipd.it) in Padua (Italy) supervised by [prof. Mauro Conti](https://www.math.unipd.it/~conti/) and with the [Ubiquitous Interaction Research Group](https://www.helsinki.fi/en/researchgroups/ubiquitous-interaction) at the [University of Helsinki](https://www.helsinki.fi/en) in Helsinki (Finland) supervised by [prof. Giulio Jacucci](https://researchportal.helsinki.fi/en/persons/giulio-jacucci).

- *Privacy* on IoT Trigger-Action Platform
- *Human-Computer Interaction* and *Identity Management* on Visors
- *Cognitive Bias* and *Heuristics* on Decision Making
